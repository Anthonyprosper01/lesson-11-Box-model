Health

Health is a holistic state of physical, mental, and social well-being, extending beyond merely being free from illness. It involves maintaining a balance in various aspects of life, including nutrition, exercise, and mental health. Good health is crucial for a high quality of life and enhances overall functioning. Lifestyle choices, such as diet and physical activity, play a significant role in health. Additionally, factors like genetics, environment, and access to healthcare can influence an individual's health status.
